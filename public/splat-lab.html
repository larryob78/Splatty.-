Where to get free splats: the samples above come from the open cakewalk/splat-data set on Hugging Face (the antimatter15 demo scenes — research-licensed, test only). For your own captures: Polycam and Luma AI both export Gaussian splats (.ply/.splat) free — scan any product with your phone, export, and drop it here. Scaniverse and SuperSplat (playcanvas) work too. Renderer: GaussianSplats3D (three.js).
