Local library: drop splat files into brand-splats-platform/data/splats/ and they appear here.
Where to get free splats: the samples above come from the open cakewalk/splat-data set on Hugging Face (the antimatter15 demo scenes — research-licensed, test only). For your own captures: Polycam and Luma AI both export Gaussian splats free — scan any product with your phone, export, and drop it here.

Higher-end source — SuperSplat gallery ↗: PlayCanvas's public gallery hosts the best-quality splats on the open web. Open any scene in the SuperSplat editor, export as .splat, and drop the file here (or into data/splats/ to keep it in the local library and run scripts/ingest.js on it). Renderer: vendored antimatter15/splat (MIT) — zero dependencies, runs offline.
